Indicators

Indicators describe the characteristics represented in the map. Select an indicator in the interactive map to explore its spatial distribution.

Building material indicators

Indicator Description
Wood Proportion of buildings associated with wood-based construction.
Masonry Proportion of buildings associated with masonry construction.
Concrete Proportion of buildings associated with concrete construction.
Steel Proportion of buildings associated with steel construction.

Interpreting values

Indicator values are displayed as percentages. Higher values indicate a greater proportion of the corresponding characteristic within the selected geographic unit.
